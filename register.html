How can I participate in Synapse?

All non-residents of DA-IICT can participate in Synapse 2019 either individually or in a contingent. The details of each method are mentioned below.

Individual Participation

Any non-resident of DA-IICT can choose to participate in Synapse 2019 individually. This can be done by purchasing the ‘Synapse 100’ pass:

Synapse 100 Pass

This pass can be purchased individually for a humble amount of Rs 100. The holder of this pass can participate in any or all of the following events:

  • Footloose: Western Dance Event.
  • Showdown: Dance faceoff
  • Naach: Eastern Dance
  • Raaga: Eastern Music
  • Rhapsody: Western Music
  • Writer's arena(Shailee): Essay writing competition
  • Poetry Slam (Shailee):  Poetry performances by artists from Spill poetry followed by a slam poetry competition judged by the artists themselves.
  • Literature Quiz (Shailee): Quiz on books, authors and everything else in this genre
  • Spell Bee (Shailee): Spelling competition
  • Headrush: Quizzing  Competition with multiple quizzes on various topics such as - MELA, General, Business-Technology and many more.
  • Crazy Math: Contest with simple questions for everyone keen about mathematics.
  • Googlock Holmes: Solve tricky riddles using search engines
  • Conceive: Photography event for everyone interested in this art
  • Forage: Best from waste
  • DA Rush: Treasure hunt within Gandhinagar
  • Acte Mono (Masquerade): Mono acting on stage
  • Comique (Masquerade): Stand-up competition

Apart from the above-mentioned events, one might register individually in the following events:

  • Synocubix: For all cubing enthusiasts. (Reg Fees: Rs 150)
  • DJ Wars: War of DJs (Reg Fees: Rs 300)

Contingent Registration

Participants might choose to register as a contingent for any of the following genre of events. Note that if a contingent has registered for a particular genre, all members of the contingent can participate in all events belonging to a genre only. The genres for which a particular contingent might want to register to include:

Masquerade (Drama) (Rs 200)

All members of the contingent will be able to participate in any one of the following events.

  • Naivete (Street Play)
  • Proscenium (Stage Play)
  • Felicity (Mime)

Note that members of this contingent can participate in other listed events with additional registration fees.

Apart from this, all members of the contingent will be able to participate in any or all of the following events:

  • Comique (Stand-up)
  • Acte Mono (Mono Acting)

Western Dance (Rs 300 per contingent)

All members of the contingent will be able to participate in the following events:

  • Footloose
  • Solo
  • Duo
  • Group
  • Showdown

Eastern Dance (Rs 250 per contingent)

All members of the contingent will be able to participate in the following events:

  • Naach
  • Solo
  • Group

Western Music (Rs 200 per contingent)

All members of the contingent will be able to participate in the following events:

  • Rhapsody
  • Battle of Bands

Eastern Music (Rs 200 per contingent)

All members of the contingent will be able to participate in the following events:

  • Raaga
  • Solo
  • Group

Rampage (Rs 800 per contingent)

Team fashion event. Budding designers express their creative independence and boldness as they illustrate their stories while the most glamorous models walk to the pumping beats.

Note: Everyone who takes accommodation on campus would receive the same benefits that a student from DA-IICT would receive.
