RAMPAGE

“I don’t do fashion, I am Fashion!” – Coco Chanel

Fashion is an art, a religion, a peek into a personality. For some, it’s an escape or a disguise while for others it’s just being comfortable.
But ultimately, fashion is an individual statement of expression for each of us and RAMPAGE is the avenue to express yourself. A fashion show is a chance for a designer to show off a particular aesthetic, a particular mood, a particular feel or point of view.
As a result, fashion shows can tend to be more conceptual and focused on a higher level idea where spectators enjoy the procession of cutting-edge ensembles.

Registration

Only team leader has to register on behalf of whole team.
Registration fee - Rs. 800 per team
NOTE- Team leader has to register on behalf of the whole team.
Students of different institutes can come together to make a team and register themselves. More than one team from same institute can register themselves.

TEAM SIZE :

  • Designers:1-4
  • Models: 8-14

  • Makeup Artists: 1-4 (excluding the models)

THEME :

“Ugly”, So what ?
Society is generally biased towards the beauty standard. A lot of people might even hold back to say it, but in their subconscious mind they have this standard for what's pretty and what's ugly. And generally everything that they label as Ugly, will be overlooked by them. This is why this time Rampage gives the opportunity for people to try and break this stereotype and show people the underlying truth, the underlying Beauty.

Rules- Footloose ‘19

Footloose is a western dance event, where participants can participate in any of the categories of Solo, Duet or Group. Each of the formats will see a prelims round, before the final round. The teams selected from the Prelims are eligible to participate in the finals.

In the prelims, the participants have to perform a part of the sequence they prepare for the Finals or the complete sequence they would perform in the finals. In any case, the performance in the prelims must be included in the performance in the finals, should the participants be eligible for the finals.

SHOWDOWN

Are you ready for some real competition. Show your creative dancing talents using props.

RULES

Eliminations

  • A prepared 2-minute performance by each participant.
  • A group of 3 participants will be given a prop each, with which they'll perform on a song for a minute.
  • Then the props will be interchanged among themselves and hence 3 such rounds of 1 minute each will be conducted between 3 participants.
  • So, the elimination round will be a total of 9 minutes for 3 participants.

Judging criteria


Choreography
Expressions
Stage presence
SCreativity in using props

NOTE

  • 2.5% of total score will be deducted for each minute above the time limit.
  • If a participant steps out of the dancing area, he/she will be warned once and on repetition, would be disqualified.
  • From all, 8 participants would qualify for the finals.

Judging criteria


Entertainment factor
Spontaneity
Creativity
Every round will be impromptu except the first point mentioned in the eliminations.
We are planning to give 2 sizzling prizes- Winner and Runner-Up.

NOTE

  • If a participant steps out of the dancing area, he/she will be penalized.
